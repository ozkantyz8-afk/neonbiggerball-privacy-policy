PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: 2024

IMPORTANT: This is an offline application. All game data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal information on external servers.

1. INTRODUCTION

NeonBiggerBall ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile game application.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect minimal information necessary to provide and improve our services:

2.1. LOCAL DATA (Stored on Your Device Only)

  • Game Statistics: Your scores, gameplay duration, game progress, unlocked themes, and settings are stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences.
  • Game Settings: Your preferences (sound, haptics, language, theme selection) are stored locally.
  • In-App Purchase Status: Whether you have purchased the "Remove Ads" feature is stored locally.

2.2. ADVERTISING DATA (Google Mobile Ads)

  • Advertising ID: Google Mobile Ads SDK may collect your device's advertising ID (AAID/IDFA) to show personalized advertisements.
  • This data is handled by Google Mobile Ads and is subject to Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • You can opt-out of personalized ads in your device settings (Settings > Google > Ads > Reset advertising ID or Opt out of Ads Personalization).

2.3. IN-APP PURCHASES

  • Purchase information is handled entirely by Google Play Store and is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • We do not have access to your payment information, credit card details, or billing address.
  • We only receive confirmation that a purchase was completed.

3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

  • To provide and improve game functionality
  • To display advertisements through Google Mobile Ads (if you have not purchased "Remove Ads")
  • To process in-app purchases through Google Play Store
  • To analyze game performance and fix bugs (locally, on your device)

4. ADVERTISING (Google Mobile Ads)

4.1. Ad Network

We use Google Mobile Ads (AdMob) to display advertisements in our game. Google Mobile Ads is a service provided by Google LLC.

4.2. Data Collection by AdMob

Google Mobile Ads may collect:

  • Device advertising ID (AAID)
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • IP address
  • Location data (approximate, based on IP)
  • App usage data

For detailed information about how Google uses data, visit: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4.3. Ad Personalization

  • Ads may be personalized based on your interests and device information.
  • You can disable personalized ads in your device settings.
  • Even with personalized ads disabled, you will still see ads, but they will be less relevant.

4.4. Opt-Out Options

  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • You can also reset your advertising ID at any time.

5. DATA STORAGE AND SECURITY

5.1. Local Storage

  • All game data (scores, settings, progress) is stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences.
  • This data never leaves your device.
  • We do not have access to this data.

5.2. No External Servers

  • We do not operate any external servers.
  • We do not collect or transmit personal information to external servers.
  • We do not use analytics services that collect personal data.

5.3. Data Security

  • Your local game data is protected by your device's security measures.
  • We recommend keeping your device updated and using screen lock protection.

6. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

6.1. Google Play Services

Our app uses Google Play Services for in-app purchases. Google Play Services data handling is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

6.2. Google Mobile Ads

Our app uses Google Mobile Ads for advertising. Google Mobile Ads data handling is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

7. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Our game is not directed to children under 13 (or under 16 in the EU). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

8. YOUR RIGHTS (GDPR Compliance)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the following rights:

8.1. Right to Access

You can access all your game data through the app's Settings menu.

8.2. Right to Rectification

You can modify your game settings at any time.

8.3. Right to Erasure

  • You can delete all game data by uninstalling the app.
  • You can reset game statistics through Settings.

8.4. Right to Data Portability

Your game data is stored locally. You can back up your device to preserve this data.

8.5. Right to Object

  • You can opt-out of personalized ads in your device settings.
  • You can purchase "Remove Ads" to disable all advertisements.

8.6. Right to Withdraw Consent

You can uninstall the app at any time to stop all data collection.

9. DATA RETENTION

  • Game data is stored locally on your device until you uninstall the app or reset game data.
  • We do not retain any data after app uninstallation.
  • Advertising data is retained by Google Mobile Ads according to Google's Privacy Policy.

10. INTERNATIONAL DATA TRANSFERS

  • Since we do not collect or transmit personal data to external servers, no international data transfers occur.
  • Advertising data collected by Google Mobile Ads may be transferred to Google's servers, which are subject to Google's Privacy Policy and data transfer agreements.
